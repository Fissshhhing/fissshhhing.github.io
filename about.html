Top Quality Fishing Lures

Explore our collection of high-performance fishing lures designed for every angler, from beginners to seasoned pros. Whether you're targeting bass, trout, or panfish, our wide variety of lures—jerkbaits, crankbaits, spinnerbaits, Senkos, and Rooster Tails—are crafted to help you land more fish with ease and confidence.

Jerkbait

A jerkbait is a slender, hard-bodied fishing lure designed to mimic an injured baitfish, often featuring a suspending or slow-floating action. Anglers retrieve it with quick jerks and pauses to trigger predatory strikes from species like bass, pike, and walleye.

Crainkbait

A crankbait is a hard-bodied lure with a plastic or metal lip that causes it to dive and wobble as it’s retrieved, imitating a swimming baitfish. It’s ideal for covering water quickly and targeting fish at various depths, especially in freshwater lakes and rivers.

Spinnerbait

A spinnerbait is a versatile lure featuring one or more spinning blades that create flash and vibration to attract fish. Its unique shape allows it to move through weeds and cover easily, making it great for targeting bass in murky or shallow water.

Senko

A Senko is a soft plastic stickbait known for its simple yet effective design that mimics a worm or baitfish. It has a natural, wiggling fall that works well for catching bass, especially when rigged weightless or wacky style.

Rooster Tail

A Rooster Tail is a type of inline spinner with a spinning blade and a feathered tail that creates flash and vibration to attract fish. It's highly effective for catching trout, bass, and panfish in both moving and still water.
